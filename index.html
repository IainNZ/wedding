Our Story

Our story begins in June 2020, as New York was waking up from its lockdown slumber. We were connected thanks to the magic of Hinge, and wine and cheese in Central Park followed that same day, continuing late into the night with roadside margaritas and "Cuomo chips". (2020 was weird!)

Over the next couple of years we moved in together, travelled, cooked, took many a long walk around the five boroughs, bought a house, and of course, welcomed our son Alastair at the end of 2022.

Now, in 2024, we'd love for you to join us for the next step in our adventure, getting married!

The Venue

Our wedding will be held at Flagstaff House, a Boulder, Colorado instituition dating from 1929. The restaurant sits in the hills above Boulder, offering great views (and food!)
